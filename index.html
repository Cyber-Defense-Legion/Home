Cybersecurity Guides

The Cybersecurity Guides project is meant to provide useful, accurate, and freely-accessible information in the form of walk-throughs, tutorials, and manuals for system administrators, developers, and end-users.

For suggestions, comments, corrections, or requests, please reach out to us at contact@cyberdefenselegion.org.

Sysadmin Utility Toolkit

The Sysadmin Utility Toolkit is a collection of Linux command-line utilities written in Ruby, bash, and x64 Intel assembly for simplifying common tasks, such as file encryption and decryption, as well as the generation of cryptographically secure pseudo-random data.

The Knowledge Base

A free knowledge base covering Mathematics, Philosophy, Physics, and Computer Science. It is not meant to replace great resources like Khan Academy, but rather picks up where they leave off, beginning with multivariable calculus, differential equations, real analysis, and abstract algebra.
